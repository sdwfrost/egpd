# Gaming and Betting Offenses in New South Wales

Counts of gaming and betting offenses recorded at 342 locations in New
South Wales, Australia.

## Usage

``` r
nsw_offenses
```

## Format

A data frame with 342 rows and 1 variable:

- offenses:

  Number of gaming and betting offenses (non-negative integer).

## Source

Australian Bureau of Statistics, New South Wales crime data.
