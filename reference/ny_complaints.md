# New York Auto Insurance Complaint Rankings

Automobile insurance company complaint data from the New York State
Department of Financial Services, covering filing years 2009–2020. Each
row represents one insurer in one filing year.

## Usage

``` r
ny_complaints
```

## Format

A data frame with 1942 rows and 4 variables:

- year:

  Filing year (2009–2020).

- upheld:

  Number of upheld complaints against the insurer.

- total:

  Total complaints filed against the insurer.

- premiums:

  Premiums written (millions of USD).

## Source

New York State Department of Financial Services, Automobile Insurance
Company Complaint Rankings, Beginning 2009.
